Welcome to Season 2402!

The Auckland Blood Bowl Federation is launching into Season 2 for 2024!

We will begin with three pre-season friendly rounds, starting on Thursday 4th July, at Hobbymaster in Penrose.

If you are interested in checking out the great game of Blood Bowl, or in joining a league, this is a great time to come have a go!

Please see the full 2401 Rules Pack

This season we are moving our league management to TourPlay. Please register at https://tourplay.net and join our the league https://tourplay.net/en/blood-bowl/abbf-season-2402

We are also moving our coach chat and banter in to the NZ Blood Bowl discord, in the #abbf channel.
Join us at https://discord.gg/bHey4w6KbB
