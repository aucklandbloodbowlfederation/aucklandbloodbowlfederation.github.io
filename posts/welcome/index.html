Welcome to the ABBF

Posted by ABBF on Mon, May 22, 2023

Hello and welcome to the Auckland Blood Bowl Federation!

This is the club page for our long running Blood Bowl league.

We run two leagues per year, starting in late Jan and finishing in early Dec. Each season consists of 2-3 preseason builder rounds, 7-8 rounds of the regular season and 1-2 playoff rounds.

Our league nights are from 6pm to 9pm on every 2nd Thursday, graciously hosted at Hobbymaster 531 Great South Road, Penrose

Our event nights are arranged on our Facebook Group, please feel free to join the group and come along.

The ABBF are proud members of the New Zealand Blood Bowl Community. Please consider joining us on the NZ Blood Bowl Facebook Group and the Discord

Thanks,
