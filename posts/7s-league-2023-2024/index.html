7s League 2023 - 2024

Posted by ABBF on Mon, Dec 11, 2023

Welcome to the first ABBF 7s league

The ABBF is pleased to announce our first ever 7s League. Blood Bowl 7s is a quick and exciting variant of Blood Bowl, played by 7 players per side, on a smaller pitch. It is great for shorter games and is a perfect way to interduce new or younger players to the game.

We will be running a 6 game season with 2 games of playoffs, for 8 games in total.

Games will be held at Hobbymaster on our League Nights on select Thursdays, over the upcomming silly season. Though games outside of these dates and played at other venues are certainly permitted.

This will be a casual league, meaning we will not have an official draw.

Following the 7s League the ABBF will be running our first ever 7s Turnament on Jan 27 2024, stay tuned for more details.

Key dates:
14/12/2023 - 7s League: Rounds 1 & 2
21/12/2023 - 7s League: Rounds 3 & 4
04/01/2024 - 7s League: Rounds 5 & 6
11/01/2024 - 7s League: Semi & Grand Finals
18/01/2024 - 11s League: Pre Season 1
27/01/2024 - 7s Tournament
01/02/2024 - 11s League: Pre-Season2
