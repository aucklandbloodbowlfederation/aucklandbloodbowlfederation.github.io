Auckland Summer Sevens Tournament 2024

Posted by ABBF on Tue, Jan 9, 2024

The ABBF proudly presents the inaugural Auckland Summer Sevens Tournament

On January 27th 2024 we will be hosting our first annual Blood Bowl Sevens tournament, as part of the NZ Blood Bowl Sevens Circuit, and in association with Hobbymaster NZ.

Come and join us for four rounds of sevens Blood Bowl in this NAF sanctioned one-day event.

Sevens is the exciting and dynamic shorter form of Nuffle’s great game and is the perfect way to introduce new players to the Blood Bowl.

Please see the official players pack for full details:
Auckland Summer 7s Players Pack

Key info:
Venue: Hobby Master Events Centre
Date: Saturday 27 January 2024 - 10am to 4:45pm
Price: $30* (Includes pizza lunch and $10 store credit per player)
Tickets: Available from the Hobbymaster Event Page
Tournament Organiser: Daniel McNicoll
